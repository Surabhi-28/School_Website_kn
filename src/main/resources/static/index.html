About Us

Where Little Steps Lead to Big Dreams

Welcome to Shree Sangam, a place where young minds grow, explore, and shine! Our kindergarten is dedicated to providing a nurturing, safe, and engaging learning environment for children to develop their creativity, confidence, and love for learning. At Shree Sangam, we believe that early childhood education is the foundation for lifelong success. Our experienced and caring teachers use fun, interactive, and hands-on learning methods to encourage curiosity and imagination

What We Do

Thanks To Get Started With Our School

Study & Game

We believe young children learn best through play. Our curriculum blends structured lessons with fun activities like storytelling, puzzles, music, and art—building critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills in an engaging way.

A to Z Programs

From literacy and numeracy to creativity and social skills, our programs support every part of a child’s growth. We build strong foundations in reading, writing, and math while nurturing imagination through art, music, and movement.

Expert Teacher

Our caring educators give each child personal attention in a warm, supportive space. Using creative teaching methods for all learning styles, they help children feel confident, curious, and ready to succeed.

Safety

Our campus is safe and child-friendly, with CCTV, secure entry points, and trained staff. Classrooms and play areas are designed to prevent accidents, and teachers ensure constant supervision for every child’s safety

Our Programs

We Offer An Exclusive Programs For Kids

English For Today

Our program focuses on correct pronunciation and accent refinement using phonics-based learning. Through phonetic drills, rhyming exercises, and speech activities, we help children develop clarity, fluency, and natural intonation. Teachers provide one-on-one guidance to ensure each child improves their speaking skills with confidence.

Arts

Children are encouraged to explore their imagination through various painting and drawing activities. From finger painting and watercolors to crayon sketches and coloring books, every child gets the opportunity to create beautiful masterpieces while improving hand-eye coordination. Our craft sessions help children develop problem-solving skills.

Physical Education

we believe in nurturing children’s physical, mental, and emotional well-being from an early age. Our Yoga Program is designed to develop flexibility, focus, maintain correct posture, coordination and balance through simple and fun yoga activities. By incorporating yoga into daily routines, we encourage a healthy and balanced lifestyle for our little learners.
